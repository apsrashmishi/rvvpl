How the zero-capex model works

We invest. You save.

The same philosophy that powers our EV charging partnerships, applied to your rooftop.

What we bring — at 100% our cost

  • Full deployment: panels, inverters, structures, and installation on your terrace
  • Ownership & operation: we own, monitor, and maintain the plant throughout its life
  • Upright execution: engineering, approvals, and commissioning handled end to end

What you bring

  • Terrace or rooftop space at your industrial or commercial premises

What you gain

  • Reduced electricity rates for the life of the project
  • Typically 15–20% savings on electricity bills
  • Green credentials — renewable power generated on your own premises

Actual savings depend on your tariff, site conditions, and consumption profile — confirmed after a free rooftop assessment.

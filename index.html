About us

Accessible charging.
Industrial-grade green power.

RashMishi VerdeVita Ventures provides electric vehicle charging and solar power solutions. We focus on offering accessible and efficient charging options for a world moving toward more sustainable mobility — and green power for industrial and commercial usage.

One philosophy runs through everything we build: zero capex for our partners. We invest, install, and operate; our partners provide the space and share the upside.

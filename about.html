About the company

Building future-ready infrastructure.

RashMishi VerdeVita Ventures Pvt. Ltd. is a Delhi-based venture investing in and operating clean-energy infrastructure — EV charging, rooftop solar, and destination hospitality — united by one zero-capex partnership philosophy.

Our three wings

One venture, three businesses

⚡

EV Charging — RV Charge

A charge point operator with 6 live charge points across South Delhi, Gurgaon, East Delhi, and Moradabad, and 4 more underway in Moradabad and Ludhiana. Beyond the network: fleet charging hubs, portable charger rentals, CMS/OCPP services for fellow CPOs, surplus power utilisation, and carbon credit trading.

☀️

Solar Power

Zero-capex rooftop and remote solar for industrial and commercial establishments under the RESCO model — with a 200 kW project underway in Moradabad. Premises owners typically save 15–20% on electricity bills.

☕

Cafes

Quality cafes for corporate locations — one operational in a premium Noida corporate building, and a charge-and-dine combo underway in Gurgaon. Seeking corporate location partners on a revenue-share model.

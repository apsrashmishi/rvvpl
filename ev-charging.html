🔌

Smart, open hardware

Beyond the plug

A full-stack charging company

RV Charge is more than a network — we offer solutions across the EV charging value chain.

🚚

Fleet charging hubs

For fleet operators and heavy users, we set up dedicated EV charging hubs at subsidized rates — reliable, high-throughput charging built around your duty cycles.

🧳

Portable chargers on rent

Heading outstation? Rent a portable charger as a safeguard for routes where charging infrastructure may fail or may not be available — and drive with confidence.

🖥️

CMS & OCPP for CPOs

Fellow charge point operators can run on our in-house charger management system with OCPP integration — plus our support centre — without investing in their own software and call centre.

🔋

Surplus power utilisation

We partner with corporates and industries to put their surplus power capacity to work — using it to set up and run EV chargers.

🌍

Carbon credit trading

The emissions our green charging avoids are quantified and monetised — we trade carbon credits, adding value for us and our partners.

🤝

Host or co-invest

Provide space and passive electrical infrastructure, contribute surplus power, or take a stake in a specific charger location.

Residential societies · RWAs · Members-only clubs

Smart, safe, zero-capex charging for your community

Society funds shouldn't have to pay for infrastructure that only some members use today. With our model, they don't — we bear the hardware and installation costs, and EV owners simply pay as they charge.

The problem with individual wiring

  • Pulling cables 50–150 m from flat meter to basement parking can cost each resident ₹30,000–50,000
  • Hazardous "spaghetti wiring" in society shafts is a fire risk
  • Personal funds spent on setups with no load management

Our community solution

  • ₹0 capex for the RWA and residents — we invest in the chargers and safe wiring
  • Surface-level installations, strictly compliant with fire safety laws
  • App-based booking: residents check availability from home — no wasted trips to the basement
  • Right-sized hardware: AC smart chargers for overnight charging, DC fast where needed
  • Pay as you charge — only for the electricity used plus a small service fee

All chargers are OCPP compliant, letting residents monitor charging status directly from their smartphones.

Co-investment

Own a stake in a specific project

We open individual projects — a single charger location, a single solar plant — to small investors who want infrastructure returns without operational involvement.

How it works

  • Pick a project: invest in a specific charger location or solar plant — not a pooled fund
  • We execute: installation, operation, and maintenance are handled entirely by us
  • You earn: profits from that project are shared with you based on your % stake

Who it suits

  • Individuals seeking exposure to EV and solar infrastructure
  • Property partners who want a larger share than the standard revenue-share model
  • Businesses wanting green assets without building an operations team

Terms, stakes, and returns are structured per project and finalised through a partnership agreement after discussion.
